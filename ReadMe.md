# CU Boulder Freshman Guide

A simple, user-friendly website designed to serve as an essential guide for incoming students at the University of Colorado Boulder. This project offers authentic, student-to-student advice across a variety of key categories related to college life.

## About

The CU Boulder Freshman Guide is created by current students at the University of Colorado Boulder. Our firsthand experience allows us to provide a more intimate and genuine perspective on navigating college life, from academics to campus resources and everything in between.

Our mission is to empower new students with clear, organized, and relevant advice that enables a seamless transition into university life and sets them up for success from day one.

## Features

The guide is organized into several key categories, each offering personal insights from our team members:

- **A Foodie's Dream** - Dining recommendations and food spots around campus
- **Touch Grass** - Outdoor activities and nature spots near Boulder
- **In Da Club(s)** - Student organizations and club involvement
- **Get Active (Or Not)** - Athletics, fitness, and recreational activities
- **MTV Cribs** - Housing and living arrangements
- **How To Get An A+ 101** - Academic tips and study strategies
- **Secret Sauce** - Insider tips and hidden gems
- **Miscellaneous** - Additional helpful information

## Usage

Simply open `index.html` in a web browser to view the guide. Navigate between the "About Us" and "Categories" pages using the header navigation. Click on any category card to view detailed student perspectives and advice.

## Technology

This is a static website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build process or dependencies are required—just open and view in any modern web browser.
